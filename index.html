Infinity Stone Collector

Welcome to Infinity Stone Collector! The rules are simple, there is a randomized number that will be displayed to you. Your goal is to make your "score" match the number provided to you below. You are given four infinity stones to choose from, each of which has a hidden and randomized value. You will click on the stones to discover their value and once you have determined their value, you must continue to click the stones in a fashion that allows your "user score" to match the number provided to you. Cheers!! Let's get to clickin'!
